Terms & Conditions

Authenticated terms and conditions for each beneficiray to receive passive income payment (PIP) systematically on ECOH PLATFORM.

Personal Savings Account (PSA) for PIP

Each ECOH PLATFORM associate’s personal account is opened once he/she effectively connects 5 new beneficiaries who all make full payments for NTD Services. LFD Services shall open the PSA in a bank for the new ECOH partner. 10,000 FCFA shall be debited from each ECOH PLATFORM partner’s total first passive income of 50,000 FCFA. 10% of the total PIP is deducted as statutory debit for sustainability of the outreach mission each time passive income is paid. Hence, 5,000 FCFA shall be debited from the first total PIP of 50,000 FCFA. It should be noted that similar 10% debits shall be done from subsequent payments. Thus, each partner will access a first net reward balance (NRB) of 35,000 FCFA.

In addition, 25,000 FCFA shall be deducted fom a PIP of 250,000 FCFA in tier two. However, for those in tier three with a PIP of 1.25 million FCFA, 10% plus 300,000 FCFA will be debited for ECOH PLATFORM's operation costs and bills. Similarly, 10% will be deducted from PIPs of 6.25 million FCFA in tier four resulting in a total cumulative net reward (CNR) balance of 6.71 million FCFA at the end of phase 1.

Community Development Statutory Investment

The minimum statutory investment (MSI) for community development is 500,000 FCFA; that is, withdrawable total CNR is 6,210,000 FCFA. Funding approved Juicy community development projects for posterity.

Beneficiaries of the CDF include: investors via dividends, owners of funded projects and the community. The procedure for authenticating project funding is done in three (03) ways: experts’ submission to the board, the CDF board’s approval and F’-Priority.

Authenticating procedure to fund projects: Softloans for personal investments for example, a center for LFD Services needs 35 million FCFA or Juicy contracts; Soft loans to purchase bulk arable land at a site whose value appreciates fast in a short period of time; Soft loans to become a NTD corporate investor given the event that the NTD demand increases; Soft loans to become a BOH product investor to boost multiple passive Income for example in VITAF, etc projects; Soft loans to become a national corporate investor (NCI) as LFD Services expands to other target nations.

ACCESS PIP & PROFITS FROM COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT FUND (CDF) VIA BANK

Personal Savings Account (PSA) for PIP from X2 ECOH PLATFORM

Each X2 ECOH PLATFORM associate’s personal account is opened once he/she effectively connects 5 new beneficiaries who all make full payments for NTD Services. LFD Services shall open the PSA in a bank for the new ECOH partner. 10,000Frs shall be debited from each X2 ECOH PLATFORM partner’s total first passive income of 50,000Frs. 10% of the total PIP is deducted as statutory debit for sustainability of the outreach mission each time passive income is paid. Hence, XAF5,000 shall be debited from the first total PIP of XAF50,000. It should be noted that similar 10% debits shall be done from subsequent payments. Thus, each partner will access a first net reward balance (NRB) of XAF35,000.

In addition, XAF25,000 shall be deducted fom a PIP of XAF250,000 in tier two. However, for those in tier three with a PIP of XAF1.25 million, 10% plus XAF300,000 will be debited for ECOH PLATFORM's operation costs and bills. Similarly, 10% will be deducted from PIPs of XAF6.25 million in tier four resulting in a total cumulative net reward (CNR) balance of XAF6.71 million at the end of phase 1.

Community Development Statutory Investment of 8% of The 4th Tier PIP

The minimum statutory investment (MSI) for community development is XAF500,000; that is, withdrawable total CNR is XAF6,210,000. Funding approved Juicy community development projects for posterity.

Beneficiaries of the CDF include: investors via dividends, owners of funded projects and the community. The procedure for authenticating project funding is done in three (03) ways: experts’ submission to the board, the CDF board’s approval and F’-Priority.

Authenticating procedure to fund projects: Softloans for personal investments for example, a center for LFD Services needs XAF35 million or Juicy contracts; Soft loans to purchase bulk arable land at a site whose value appreciates fast in a short period of time; Soft loans to become a NTD corporate investor given the event that the NTD demand increases; Soft loans to become a BOH product investor to boost multiple passive Income for example in VITAF, etc projects; Soft loans to become a national corporate investor (NCI) as LFD Services expands to other target nations.
